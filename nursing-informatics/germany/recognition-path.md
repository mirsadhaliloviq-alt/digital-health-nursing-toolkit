# Germany Nursing Recognition Path

## Overview

This document summarizes the nursing recognition pathway in Germany for internationally educated nurses.

## Current Status

* Bachelor of Nursing completed
* MSc Digital Healthcare completed
* German language learning in progress
* Recognition process initiated in Bavaria
* Employment pathway linked to a German healthcare institution

## Recognition Process

### Step 1: Qualification Assessment

Submission of:

* Diploma
* Diploma Supplement
* Transcript of Records
* Identification Documents
* Certified Translations

### Step 2: Recognition Decision

Possible outcomes:

* Full Recognition
* Deficit Notice (Defizitbescheid)

### Step 3: Compensation Measures

Depending on the assessment:

* Adaptation Period (Anpassungslehrgang)
  or
* Knowledge Examination (Kenntnisprüfung)

### Step 4: Language Requirements

Target:

* German B1/2 level

Focus areas:

* Medical terminology
* Clinical communication
* Nursing documentation

### Step 5: Professional Recognition

After successful completion:

* State Recognition
* Nursing License (Urkunde)
* Professional Registration

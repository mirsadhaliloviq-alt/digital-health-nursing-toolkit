## Master's Thesis

### Assessment of Artificial Intelligence Readiness Among Nursing Students in Kosovo (MAIRS-MS)

**Author:** Mirsad Haliloviq

**Degree:** MSc Digital Healthcare

**Institution:** Kolegji Heimerer

**Year:** 2026

### Research Areas

- Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare
- Nursing Education
- Digital Health
- Nursing Informatics
- Healthcare Innovation

### Methodology

- Cross-sectional study
- MAIRS-MS instrument
- 320 nursing students
- Quantitative analysis

### Key Findings

- Positive overall AI readiness
- Strong ethical awareness
- Positive vision regarding AI adoption
- Need for additional practical AI education

# AI Readiness Research in Nursing Education (MAIRS-MS)

## Overview

This section contains research materials related to Artificial Intelligence readiness among nursing students in Kosovo.

## MSc Thesis

**Title:**

Assessment of Artificial Intelligence Readiness among Nursing Students in Kosovo (MAIRS-MS)

## Research Design

- Quantitative study
- Cross-sectional design
- Sample: 320 nursing students
- Instrument: MAIRS-MS

## Key Findings

| Dimension | Mean Score |
|------------|------------|
| Ethics | 4.32 |
| Vision | 4.18 |
| Cognition | 3.12 |
| Ability | 3.05 |
| Overall Readiness | 3.67 |

## Main Conclusion

Nursing students in Kosovo demonstrate positive attitudes toward Artificial Intelligence and high ethical awareness, but require additional education and practical training to improve AI-related knowledge and competencies.

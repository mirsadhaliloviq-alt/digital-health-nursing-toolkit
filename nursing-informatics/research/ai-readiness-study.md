# AI Readiness of Nursing Students

## Overview

This project summarizes research related to the readiness of nursing students to use Artificial Intelligence in healthcare and nursing practice.

## Research Focus

- Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare
- Nursing Education
- AI Readiness
- Digital Health
- Healthcare Innovation

## Objectives

- Assess AI readiness among nursing students.
- Explore attitudes toward AI in healthcare.
- Identify educational needs for AI integration in nursing curricula.

## Keywords

AI, Nursing, Healthcare, Digital Health, Nursing Informatics, Education, Innovation.

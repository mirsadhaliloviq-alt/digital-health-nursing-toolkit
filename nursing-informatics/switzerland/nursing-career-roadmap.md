s# Switzerland Nursing Career Roadmap

## Overview

This document outlines a long-term professional pathway toward nursing and digital health opportunities in Switzerland.

## Current Profile

* Bachelor of Nursing
* MSc Digital Healthcare
* Research interest in AI and Digital Health
* International healthcare background
* German language development

## Short-Term Goals

### Germany

* Complete nursing recognition process
* Obtain professional license (Urkunde)
* Gain clinical experience
* Achieve German B2 level or higher

## Medium-Term Goals

### Switzerland

* Apply for recognition through the Swiss Red Cross (SRK)
* Prepare professional documentation
* Gain additional clinical experience
* Explore healthcare technology opportunities

## Career Areas of Interest

### Nursing

* Acute Care Nursing
* Internal Medicine
* Rehabilitation
* Community Care

### Digital Health

* Nursing Informatics
* Clinical Informatics
* Digital Health Projects
* Healthcare Innovation

### Healthcare Technology

* Telemedicine
* Health Information Systems
* Electronic Health Records
* AI in Healthcare

## Long-Term Vision

Combine clinical nursing knowledge with digital healthcare expertise to contribute to healthcare innovation, nursing informatics, and technology-enabled patient care.

This roadmap serves as a personal professional development plan for future opportunities in Switzerland.
